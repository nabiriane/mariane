I am a UX designer that enjoys solving problems and turning solutions into effective and beautiful products. I, firstly, did a BA in Creative Pattern Making in South of France which helped me being familiar with vector drawing and develop a keen eye for detail. After my first internship as a pattern maker, I realised that I wanted to explore more disruptive and technological products and turned myself to the UX Immersive course at General Assembly.

After an initial experience as the first UX Designer at FX Digital, I decided to come back on campus to work as an Instructor Associate. I had the chance to work alongside three different Senior Designers and Lead Instructors. I then decided to join the Experience Team at Hugo&Cat where I have the great pleasure to work alongside talented people on challenging and compelling projects for a year and a half. Following that experience, I joined the brilliant UX team at Brilliant Basics where I currently work.
